Fun Facts
Cameras

Manos was filmed with cameras that could only film for 32 seconds at a time, leading to a lot of editing issues.

Copyright

The movie was never copyrighted, so it is freely available and easily accessible. An unrestored version can be found on the Wikipedia page.
