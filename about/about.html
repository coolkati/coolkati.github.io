About me

 I am a Web Development student who is graduating from Alfred State College in May 2022. Like many people I know, my interest in programming developed from a serious interest in gaming. I decided to specialize in web development as it combines my interest in programming with my enjoyment of design and aesthetics. As a frequent internet user, I know what users like, what they find frustrating, and what good design should look like. Now, I would like to use my knowledge of web design and web technologies to make visually appealing and functional sites.
